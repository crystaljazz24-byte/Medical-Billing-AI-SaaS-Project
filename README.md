# Medical Billing AI SaaS

## Overview

This is a **Medical Billing AI SaaS** built with **Next.js**. It helps clinics automate:

- CPT → ICD coding suggestions based on **specialty and patient age**  
- Fee schedules and maximum payouts for **Medicare and commercial payors**  
- Claim generation in **HCFA-1500 / UB-04 format**  
- Payor contacts and **push-button claim submission**  
- Subscription payments using **Stripe**

---

## Features

- **AI-Powered Coding:** Suggests the most common CPT → ICD combinations per specialty.  
- **Age & Specialty Specific:** Coding recommendations adapt to patient age and specialty.  
- **Payor Guidelines:** Pages for Medicare and commercial payor rules.  
- **Fee Schedules:** Shows CMS and commercial payor maximum payouts.  
- **Claims Submission:** Generates electronic claims and shows payor contact info.  
- **Dashboard:** Track claim status, patient info, and billing history.  
- **Stripe Integration:** Manage subscriptions and SaaS access.
